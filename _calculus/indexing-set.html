Let $I$ be a box and $P$ a partition of $I$. Then the associated indexing set is $J:=\{1, \dots, l_1\}\times\dots\tiimes\{1, \dots, l_n\}$.
Let $\vec{a}=(a_1, \dots, a_n)\in J$. $\vec{a}$ is a multiindex of $J$.
