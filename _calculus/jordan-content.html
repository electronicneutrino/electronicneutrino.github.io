Let $\chi_S:\mathbb{R}^n\to\mathbb{R}$ be the characteristic function of a set $S\subseteq\mathbb{R}^n$. We say that $S$ has (Jordan) content if $\chi_S$ is integrable on $S$ and the volume is $$\begin{align*} \mu(S):=\int_S\chi_S(\vec{x})d\vec{x}. \end{align*}$$
