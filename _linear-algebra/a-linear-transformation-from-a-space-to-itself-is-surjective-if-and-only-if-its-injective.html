--- layout: equivalence title: Injective, Surjective, Invertible equivalences ---
Let $V$ be a finite dimensional vector space and $T:V\to V$ be a linear transformation. The following are equivalent
  1. $T$ is invertible
  2. $T$ is injective
  3. $T$ is surjective
  4. if $\{v_1, \dots, v_n\}$ is a basis for $V$, then $\{Tv_1, \dots, Tv_n\}$ is a basis for $V$.
