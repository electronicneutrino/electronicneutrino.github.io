Let $V$, $W$ be finite dimensional vector spaces over $\mathbb{F}$. Let $B$ and $C$ be the basis for $V$ and $W$, and $T:V\to W$ be linear. Then $$\begin{align*} [T]_{C\leftarrow B}[v]_B=[Tv]_c \end{align*}$$
