A subset $B\subseteq V$ is a basis for $V$ if
  1. $V=\text{span }B$
  2. $B$ is linearly independent
That is, a basis for $V$ is the minimal spanning set for $V$.

Concepts

If $S\subseteq V$ is linearly independent, then $S$ is a basis for $\text{span }S$.
