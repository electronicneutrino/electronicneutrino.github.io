Let $V$ be a finite dimensional space over $\mathbb{F}$, where $\mathbb{F}$ is algebraically complete. Then for any linear transformation $T:V\to V$, $\text{det}T$ is the product of the eigenvalues of $T$, and $\text{tr}(T)$ is the sum of the eigenvalues of $T$.
