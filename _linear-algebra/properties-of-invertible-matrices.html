Properties of invertible matrices

Let $A$ and $B$ be $n\times n$ functions.
  1. If $A$ is invertible, then so is $A^{-1}$ and $(A^{-1})^{-1}=A$
  2. If $A$ and $B$ are both invertible then so is $AB$ and $(AB)^{-1}=B^{-1}A^{-1}$
