If $A$ is a $n\times n$ matrix, then we can define its powers as $$\begin{align*} A^0&= I \\ A^1 &= A \\ A^2 &=A\cdot A \\ \vdots\\ A^n &=\underbrace{A\cdot \dots \cdot A}_{n\text{ times}} \end{align*}$$
